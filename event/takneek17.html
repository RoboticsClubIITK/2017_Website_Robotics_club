Takneek' 17

The Fight to Unite!


The Science and Technology Council presents to you Takneek 2017, the intra-IITK Science and Technology Championship. A competition in which all the pools shall participate with one common aim in mind, winning the coveted Takneek trophy, with their favourite Marvel Superhero by their side. So, people get your mental circuits whirring at top speed, and start thinking, innovating and designing. Because in a competition of the best, only the spectacular wins!
