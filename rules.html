Rules and Regulations

 Keep all the tools and components back at their place.

 Respect club property and don't damage it.

 Make sure, when you leave this room, all the worktables are clean and empty.

 If you find something missing in club, inform us. We will be glad to help you out.

 No eatables inside club.

 You can't take any tools or components out of club unless issued to you.

 Don't play with the tools. You may harm yourself.

 Are you last to leave this room??? Switch off the lights and hand over the keys of club to concerned person.

 No movies and games on computer.

Be Responsible. Let's learn and grow together. Violators may be fined and/or expelled from club activities.
